# 5.2 Relationship Briefing

**Area:** 5. Daily Experience

Before a meeting, date, call, or event, the app prepares a concise briefing about the person.

## Capabilities

- Show recent notes.
- Show open promises.
- Show sensitive topics.
- Show current life events.
- Show suggested topics.
- Show things to avoid.
- Suggest questions to ask.
- Suggest personal touches.

## Example

You are seeing your sister tomorrow. She recently mentioned stress at work and wanting help moving. You promised to send her a mover recommendation.

## Possible Data Objects

- `Briefing`
- `BriefingContext`
- `BriefingItem`

## Implementation Notes

Briefings can be triggered manually or by calendar integration later.
